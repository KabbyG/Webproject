Community Work

SBC is highly dedicated in assisting the community in collaboration with Mtwara Volunteering Crew (MVC). MVC is an international charity group founded by one of our Director’s Amin and his Wife.

Empowering Women through small scale industries Raising HIV/Aids awareness and supporting its victims Education for orphans Supporting a local orphanage in Mtwara municipality Collaborating, hosting and acting as a facilitator for other International charity groups, a recent one being SA Adventures from South Africa.
